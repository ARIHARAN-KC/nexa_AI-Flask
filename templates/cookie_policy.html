Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as maintaining your login session.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences, like dark mode.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are used to:

  • Ensure the website operates smoothly.
  • Analyze traffic and improve our services.
  • Enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but this may impact your ability to use certain features of our Service. For more information, consult your browser's help section.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics) that set their own cookies. These are subject to the respective third parties' privacy policies.
